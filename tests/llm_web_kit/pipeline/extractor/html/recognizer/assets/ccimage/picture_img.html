Chicken in curry sauce

Easy to make recipe with chicken, curry sauce and rice
Written by Tomas Lyngen
Published: 19 Nov 2022

Ingredients

  • 4-6 pieces of chicken
  • 1 tbps butter or oil for frying
  • 100-200 g vegetables (optional), frozen works fine

Sauce

  • 6 dl milk or cream
  • 2 dl ketchup
  • 5 tsp curry
  • 2 tbsp vinegar (optional)

Sauce seasoning

  • 3 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pepper

Served along

  • 3-4 scope Rice

Directions

Fry chicken

Add some butter or oil in a pan, then fry the chicken lightly on both sides until golden. Chicken will be added to the sauce later, so no need to cook through

Mix sauce

In a casserole, add the ingredients for the sauce and mix together

Add seasoning

Now you add spices, here we recommend season to taste

Add chicken and cook

Now add the chicken and cook on medium heat for about 10 minutes

Add vegetables (optional)

We recommend adding vegetables, then cook for another 10-15 minutes

Serve with rice

Now it's time to sit down and enjoy the meal
